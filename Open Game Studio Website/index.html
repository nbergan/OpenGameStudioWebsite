Get Started

Design

Let your creativity flow as you design new levels, 3D models, sound effects and more for a real video game in active development! With Open Game Studio, everyone has a chance to share their creations with the world through the games we release on platforms like Steam, Game Jolt, and more.

Connect

Experience a welcoming and encouraging community of people passionate about every aspect of game creation. Whether you are interested in game design or programming, 3d modeling or audio engineering, Open Game Studio offers you a place to engage with others in positive, meaningful ways.

Grow

We want to see your skills grow! Take your abilities to the next level by participating in a real studio environment. Learn and improve invaluable skills in teamwork, agile development, and collaborative work across multiple modern tools and a large, long-term game development projects.
